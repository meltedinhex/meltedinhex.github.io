**Melted in Hex** is a malware analysis and reverse-engineering notebook — where binaries melt down to hex.

Here you'll find deep dives into real-world malware: ransomware teardowns, packer and obfuscation analysis, FLARE-On / CTF write-ups, and hands-on reversing of nasty samples down to their bytes. Increasingly, that work meets **AI in security** — using and scrutinising machine intelligence where it helps (and where it doesn't) in modern threat analysis.

Reverse engineering threats down to their bytes, where malware analysis meets AI.

These posts were originally published on Blogger and have been migrated here, preserving their original publication dates.
